## **🔤 Typography: Intelligence + Tradition**

### **Three-Font Strategy**

**Academic Hierarchy (Libre Baskerville serif):**
- `academicDisplay` - Major titles, ceremonial content
- `academicTitle` - Section headers, important announcements  
- `academicSubtitle` - Traditional academic content

**Neural Interface (Inter sans):**
- `neuralDisplay` - AI-powered feature headers
- `neuralTitle` - Intelligence system titles
- `neuralSubtitle` - Neural network content

**Teacher Efficiency (Inter optimized):**
- `teacherHeading` - Quick-scan section headers
- `teacherLabel` - Form labels, navigation
- `teacherBody` - Main content, readable at classroom distances
- `teacherCaption` - Secondary info, status indicators

**Why This Works:**
- **Serif for tradition** - honors academic heritage  
- **Sans for efficiency** - optimal teacher scanning
- **Clear hierarchy** - teachers find information instantly
- **Cultural support** - Telugu integration built-in
