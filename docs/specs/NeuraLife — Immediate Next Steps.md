## Step 1 — Company Registration (Private Limited)

**Why Private Limited, not LLP or Sole Proprietorship:**

- LLP cannot issue equity to investors — kills your seed round before it starts
- Sole proprietorship has unlimited personal liability — one lawsuit and it is your personal assets
- Private Limited: investor-friendly, limited liability, professional credibility with school chains whose trustees have legal teams

**Exact steps:**

| Action | Who | Cost | Time |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Get Digital Signature Certificate (DSC) for you as director | CA / online | ₹1,500–2,500 | 1–2 days |
| Name reservation on MCA portal | CA | Included | 2–3 days |
| File SPICe+ form (incorporation) | CA | ₹8,000–15,000 all-in | 5–7 working days |
| Receive Certificate of Incorporation (COI) | MCA | — | Day 7–10 |
| Apply for PAN + TAN (auto-generated with COI now) | — | Free | Same day as COI |
| Open current account | Any bank | ₹0–5,000 min balance | 3–5 days |
| **Total cost** |  | **₹10,000–22,000** | **2–3 weeks** |

**Company name options to check:**

- NeuraLife Technologies Private Limited
- NeuraLife EdTech Private Limited
- NeuraLife AI Private Limited

Check availability at: mca.gov.in → MCA services → Check company name

**CA recommendation:**
Use an online CA service (Razorpay Rize, LegalWiz, Vakilsearch) rather than a traditional CA for incorporation — faster, cheaper, same legal outcome. Cost: ₹8,000–12,000 all-in.

**What to have ready before calling the CA:**

- Your Aadhaar + PAN (for director KYC)
- Registered office address (your home address is fine for v1)
- Company name (3 options in order of preference)
- Authorised capital: ₹10 lakh (standard starting point)
- Paid-up capital: ₹1 lakh (you and no co-founder = 100% shareholding)

**Action this week:** Call or WhatsApp two CA services. Get quotes. Choose. Start the process.

## Step 2 — GST Registration

**Why GST before the first invoice:**
Any invoice you issue without GST registration is non-compliant. A school chain's accounts team will not process a payment without a valid GSTIN on the invoice. This blocks your first payment even if the sale is closed.

**GST classification for NeuraLife:**

| Revenue line | GST applicability | Rate |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Platform subscription (EdTech) | **Likely exempt** under SAC 9992 (education services) — but verify with CA | 0% if exempt |
| SmartPad hardware | **Taxable** — electronic device | 18% |
| Content licensing (v3) | Check at time | TBD |

**Critical note:** The GST exemption for education services is nuanced. Formal school education is exempt. An EdTech subscription may or may not be. Get a written opinion from your CA before issuing your first invoice. If taxable: ₹500/student becomes ₹590 (pass-through to parent, not absorbed by NeuraLife).

**Steps:**

| Action | Platform | Cost | Time |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Register on GST portal | gst.gov.in | Free | 1–3 working days |
| Upload: PAN, Aadhaar, COI, bank statement, address proof | — | — | — |
| Receive GSTIN | — | Free | 3–7 days |
| File Nil returns monthly until first invoice | — | Free (DIY) or ₹500/month (CA) | Ongoing |

**Do this immediately after receiving COI.** GST registration can run in parallel with company incorporation (takes 3–7 days after COI).

This is the most important 10 weeks of the entire project. The demo is not a prototype. It is a working product with real data that a principal and two teachers use in a 90-minute session without you explaining every screen.

## Step 5 — Founding Partner Pitch Deck

**Format:** 8 slides. PowerPoint or Google Slides. Clean, not flashy. The product is impressive enough — the deck does not need to be.

**Audience:** School chain trustee / owner. Business-minded. Sceptical but open.

**Slide structure:**

```
Slide 1 — The Problem (1 image, 3 numbers)
  "India has 250 million school students.
   A teacher knows a student is falling behind after a failed exam.
   That is 4 months too late."

Slide 2 — The Gap (contrast)
  Left: What schools have today (ERP + intuition)
  Right: What students need (real-time intelligence)

Slide 3 — NeuraLife (one sentence + product screenshot)
  "NeuraLife is the learning intelligence platform for AP and Telangana schools."
  [Screenshot: School Health Dashboard — looks real, looks impressive]

Slide 4 — How It Works (4-step loop)
  Student writes on SmartPad → AI analyses → Teacher gets alert →
  Parent stays informed → Student improves

Slide 5 — What Your School Gets (before/after)
  Before: AT_RISK identified in 4–6 weeks
  After: Identified in 3 days
  Before: Parents informed at exam results
  After: Parents informed every evening in Telugu

Slide 6 — The Technology (for credibility, not for detail)
  On-device AI (no internet needed) | Telugu + English | SCERT content |
  Replaces your ERP | Complete school management | SmartPad included

Slide 7 — The Founding Partner Offer
  "6 months. Free. At one branch.
   SmartPads for every student.
   Full platform for every teacher.
   No obligation. Just results."
  [3 bullet points: what you get / what we get / what happens after]

Slide 8 — Next Step (single call to action)
  "Can we visit your school next week?"
  Your photo + name + mobile + email
```

**Length of pitch meeting:** 30 minutes max for the deck. 30 minutes for demo. 30 minutes for questions. **Total: 90 minutes.**

**Do not email the deck without a meeting.** A deck without a human presenting it is a document that gets forwarded to the wrong person and forgotten. Book the meeting first. Bring the deck to the meeting.
